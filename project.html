ROSE

Damascus Rose

Original country: Syria

The appearance of the rose suggests hope, self-confidence, harmony, tranquility, and depth of contemplation.
The rose symbolizes the concepts of love and sincere emotions, and indicates feelings of nobility and eternity.

TULIPA

The curse

Original country:Middle Asia

The tulip flower is known as the flower of love and fidelity because of the Iranian legend that says that there was a young man named “Farhard” who received news of his lover called “Shirin.”His intense sadness and love for her prompted him to throw himself from the top of a mountain with his horse, and he bled profusely and a place bloomed. Every drop of his blood is a tulip.

LAVANDULA

Launda

Original country:North Africa and the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean

The lavender flower symbolizes purity, uniqueness, devotion, serenity and elegance, and purple is classified as a royal color that embodies elegance, purity and luxury.
